Nintendo Entertainment System (NES)

The Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), released in 1985, is one of the most iconic gaming consoles in history. The NES brought the video game industry back to life after the crash of 1983 with its innovative design and legendary game library. Classic games like Super Mario Bros., The Legend of Zelda, and Metroid were introduced on this platform, setting the standard for future video games. With its robust hardware and memorable titles, the NES solidified Nintendo's place as a leader in the gaming industry and remains a nostalgic favorite among gamers.
